## Quick Reference Card

**Toggle Effect:** Alt + I  
**Open Settings:** Click toolbar icon  
**Default Mode:** Green NV  
**Config Location:** `GameData/KerbVisionIR/PluginData/settings.cfg`  

**Quick Settings:**
- Brightness: 0.6 (default)
- Contrast: 0.55 (default)
- Gamma: 0.5 (default)
- Tint Strength: 0.7 (default)
